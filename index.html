History

Started in 1962, Nonna's Cibo Casalingo was first started with Nonna Adelina cooking out of her kitchen for the neighborhood children, until she realized that more than just the kids enjoyed her food. After purchasing a small restaurant in the suburbs of New York City, the love she cooked with flourished in her business. Now ran by Nonna's daughter Elena, Nonna's love continues to flow through each person who tastes the authentic Italian cooking. Nonna believed in three things; Love, family and food and at Nonna's Cibo Casalingo, you will see all three.
